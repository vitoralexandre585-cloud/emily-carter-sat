How It Works

Three steps to test day.

1

Diagnostic session

We start with a full SAT under real timed conditions. I'm not chasing the number, I'm watching where your child slows down, second-guesses, or misreads. That's what the score alone can't show you.

2

Targeted sessions

Each session addresses specific patterns identified in the diagnostic. I send a short written note after every session so parents know exactly what we worked on and what to practice before the next meeting.

3

Test-day readiness

The final sessions are about rhythm, pacing, and confidence, not cramming. Students who've worked with me consistently say test day felt familiar, not frightening.
